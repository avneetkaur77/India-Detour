SOMNATH

The Somnath temple, also called Somanātha temple or Deo Patan, is a Hindu temple located in Prabhas Patan, Veraval in Gujarat, India.
It is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites for Hindus and is believed to be first among the twelve jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva

Route Information

Landmarks

By Road : Somnath is well-connected to virtually all of Gujarat's major cities by government and commercial bus services. It is around 82 kilometres from Junagadh, 270 kilometres from Bhavnagar, and 120 kilometres from Porbandar.

Nearest Airport : The nearest airport to Somnath is Keshod, which is around 50 kilometres away. Keshod is served by daily flights from Mumbai and other cities.

Nearest Railway Station : The nearest railway station is Veraval, 7 kilometres from Somnath. A daily train runs from Veraval to Ahmedabad, while other cities such as Jabalpur and Pune also have frequent train services. It is certainly one of the easiest ways if you're looking at how to go to Somnath.


Timings & Ticket

The Somnath Temple darshan tickets are not required for live darshan of main deity. Also there are not any Somnath Temple VIP Darshan or aarti facility for devotees by the somnath trust

Best time to visit Somnath

Wintertime is the best season for Somnath. The Somnath weather stays pleasant and suitable for touring. October, November, December, January, February, and March are the finest months to visit.
The climate is nice, with Somnath temperature ranging from 10°C to 25°C.

Local places to visit nearby

1) Somnath Beach
This beach, only a short walk from the Somnath Temple, is well-known for its cleanliness and stunning views of the sea. Though swimming is not suggested owing to the unpredictable nature of the tides, you may relax and enjoy the cool sea wind and natural splendour.

2) Suraj Mandir
dedicated to the Sun God, is located in Somnath on the northern end of the Triveni Ghat. Mornings are ideal for visiting and admiring the temple's architectural splendour because it faces the rising sun.
